# CAr sellers 

Build a modern SaaS web platform and mobile-friendly application for car dealers and private car sellers. The platform helps users create professional studio-quality car photos automatically using AI. Humanity looked at expensive car photography studios and decided: “What if we replace all that with algorithms and monthly subscriptions?” So here we are. Honestly, not the worst idea humans have had this decade.

---

# Project Name

**AutoStudio AI**
(Use this as temporary branding. Modern, luxury automotive feeling.)

---

# Main Goal

The platform allows users to:

1. Upload a photo of a car taken with a mobile phone
2. Automatically remove the original background using AI
3. Place the car into a selected professional virtual studio environment
4. Generate realistic, high-end dealership-style photos
5. Export/download final images in high quality
6. Manage projects, studios, branding, and subscriptions

The final result should look like:

* A professional automotive photographer took the photo
* The car is inside a luxury showroom or studio
* Lighting, shadow, reflections, and perspective are realistic

The AI compositing quality is the MOST IMPORTANT feature.

# 8. AI Quality Requirements

VERY IMPORTANT:

The generated image must:

* Look realistic
* Preserve original car proportions
* Preserve paint color accuracy
* Preserve wheel details
* Preserve reflections
* Match lighting with studio

AI should avoid:

* Warped wheels
* Fake reflections
* Broken shadows
* Distorted body panels
* Unrealistic perspective

Use advanced segmentation and compositing pipeline.

Suggested AI stack:

* Background removal AI
* Object segmentation
* Diffusion enhancement
* Shadow synthesis
* Reflection rendering
* Perspective matching
